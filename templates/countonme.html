Features

Privacy-first app

Your data is stored only on your phone and remains there. Nothing is transfered to the internet except if you export your data on your own. Check our privacy policy.

Free of ads

There are no ads in the app. No bandwidth stealing and no delays due to ad loading. Your focus stays on counting.

Full customizable

Create many different types of counters and organize them using tags. Set counter label, color, icon, sound, description, etc.

Various effects available

Notification of counter change via feedback such as sound effect, phonelight flash, screen flash, vibration etc.

Rich data and statistics

See details of your counters. Follow your progress with graphical progress bar. Compare increments and decrements with pie chart.

Advanced features

Reset counter and log changes. Lock counter. Export and import counter data.

Free an premium

All features and functionalities are freely available in the basic version. However, you may support our efforts by buying Premium version - one time payment for a lifetime. Thank you in advance.
